QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

If you have questions, I have answers.

Why does the Commission Editor have an Estimate Range instead of a final price?

The Commission Editor lets you customize your commission request. However, the price you get from a single change is NOT accurate enough to give a strong estimate. To fix this, I made the Estimate Range, which shows two prices:

- The lower price is the total without the Complexity Fee.
- The higher price is the total with the maximum Compelxity Fee.

The prices you get from the Estimate Range will never exceed more than the maximum.

If your commissions are SFW, why are there NSFW artist characters in the Portfolio?

The commissions I do are SFW. However, that doesn't mean NSFW artists can't ask for a SFW commission. However, I know this opens a problem for users under 18 who view the site and follow credit links back to NSFW artists who don't have SFW accounts or safe-for-all-ages links. To keep users safe, I provided 2 warnings in the Portfolio for characters who belong to NSFW artists who don't have SFW or safe-for-all-ages links.
